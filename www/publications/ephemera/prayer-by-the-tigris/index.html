Prayer by the Tigris

A Prayer by the Tigris; 19 March 2003 (2003) Kim Stafford 7.5 x 5 inches, 4 pages 220 numbered copies signed by the author Handset Bembo types Mulberry paper Single folio pamphlet sewn to a paper tri-fold cover made of Canson Mi-Teintes The right fold of the cover sits on top and is torn.

Dependence Day

By John Daniel

Art by Margot Voorhies

Fall 2007

11.5 by 18 inches.

Edition size 103

Numbered and signed by both the poet and the artist.

Printed on black Somerset Velvet paper. The text type is handset Perpetua with the display type Charlemagne and printed from a polymer plate. Both the type and graphic were printed on a Vandercook 219 proofing press. The top of the graphic was brushed silver pearl dry pigment as it came off the press.
